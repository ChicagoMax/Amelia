AMELIA MORROW

Amelia Morrow is an American born artist, actress/singer/dancer, and gymnast. Born in Evanston, Il., she was raised in Chicago's Rogers Park neighborhood where she was influenced by the neighborhood's ethnic diversity.

In 2018, Amelia's athletic pursuits led her to compete in Chicago Park District "City Wide" gymnastics meet. She had placed fifth overall and third in the beam in her regional meet.

Artistically, Amelia continues to pursue multi-media projects. Her installation pieces include original drawings and sculptures creating an atmosphere that transports the observer to a nostalgic Americana. In "Cozy Corner", we step inside a modern day Robert Frank photograph, reminding us of the comforts of the neighborhood diner.
